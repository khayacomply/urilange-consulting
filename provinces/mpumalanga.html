Business Registration Services in Mpumalanga

We provide comprehensive business registration and compliance services across the Mpumalanga province. From Nelspruit to Witbank, Middelburg to Secunda, we're here to help your business succeed in this province known for mining, agriculture, and tourism.

Key Industries in Mpumalanga

  • Coal Mining - South Africa's largest coal producer in Witbank
  • Energy Production - Eskom power stations generate 80% of SA's electricity
  • Steel & Ferrochrome - Middelburg steel industry hub
  • Petrochemicals - Sasol Secunda operations
  • Tourism - Kruger Park gateway, Panorama Route, Blyde River Canyon
  • Agriculture - Subtropical fruits, nuts, and forestry

Popular Services: Mining contractor registration, COIDA for mining and energy, CIDB for construction, B-BBEE for mining charter compliance, VAT for agricultural exports.

Business Opportunities in Mpumalanga

Mpumalanga powers South Africa and offers unique opportunities:

  • Mining Support Services - Coal mining contractors and suppliers
  • Energy Sector - Eskom contractors and renewable energy
  • Mpumalanga Provincial Tenders - Government procurement
  • Kruger Tourism - Safari lodges and tour operators
  • Mozambique Border Trade - Komatipoort border post

We help you: Register on CSD, prepare tender documents, obtain B-BBEE certificates, and comply with mining and energy sector regulations.
