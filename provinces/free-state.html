We provide comprehensive business registration and compliance services across the Free State province. From Bloemfontein to Welkom, Kroonstad to Bethlehem, we're here to help your business succeed in South Africa's heartland.

Business Opportunities in Free State

The Free State offers unique opportunities for entrepreneurs:

  • Agricultural Value Chain - Farming, processing, distribution
  • Mining Support Services - Gold mining sector suppliers
  • Free State Provincial Tenders - Government procurement
  • Mangaung Metro Tenders - Bloemfontein municipality
  • Tourism Development - Golden Gate, Clarens, Parys
  • Renewable Energy - Solar and wind projects

We help you: Register on CSD, prepare tender documents, obtain B-BBEE certificates, and comply with all agricultural and mining regulations.

Business Challenges in Free State & How We Help

Our Solution: We specialize in agricultural business registrations including UIF, COIDA, and sector-specific compliance.

Mining contractors need specific registrations and compliance with Mine Health and Safety Act.

Our Solution: We handle CIDB registration for mining contractors and ensure full COIDA compliance.
