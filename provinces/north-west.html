Business Registration Services in North West

We provide comprehensive business registration and compliance services across the North West province. From Rustenburg to Potchefstroom, Klerksdorp to Brits, we're here to help your business succeed in this province known for platinum mining, agriculture, and tourism.

Key Industries in North West

  • Platinum Mining - World's largest platinum reserves in Rustenburg and Brits
  • Gold Mining - Historic goldfields in Klerksdorp
  • Agriculture - Maize, sunflowers, cattle farming, and game ranching
  • Tourism - Sun City, Pilanesberg Game Reserve, Madikwe
  • Education - North-West University (Potchefstroom)
  • Manufacturing - Automotive components and mining equipment

Popular Services: Mining contractor registration, B-BBEE for mining charter compliance, COIDA for mining safety, CIDB for construction, agricultural business registration.

Business Opportunities in North West

North West is South Africa's platinum powerhouse with unique opportunities:

  • Platinum Belt Mining - World's largest platinum producer
  • North West Provincial Tenders - Government procurement
  • Ngaka Modiri Molema Tenders - District municipality
  • Sun City Tourism - Entertainment and hospitality sector
  • Botswana Border Trade - Ramatlabama border post
  • Mining Support Services - Equipment and contractors

We help you: Register on CSD, prepare tender documents, obtain B-BBEE certificates, and comply with mining charter and agricultural regulations.

Business Challenges in North West & How We Help

Mining Charter Compliance

Mining businesses must comply with the Mining Charter and specific sector regulations for B-BBEE and ownership.

Our Solution: We specialize in mining sector B-BBEE certificates, CIDB registration for mining contractors, and full compliance documentation.

Mining Safety Requirements

Mining contractors face strict health and safety requirements under the Mine Health and Safety Act.

Our Solution: We handle COIDA registration, safety compliance documentation, and all mining sector-specific requirements.
