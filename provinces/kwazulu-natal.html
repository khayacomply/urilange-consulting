Business Registration Services in KwaZulu-Natal

We provide comprehensive business registration and compliance services across the KwaZulu-Natal province. From the bustling port city of Durban to Pietermaritzburg and Richards Bay, we support businesses throughout this dynamic region.

Key Industries in KwaZulu-Natal

  • Port & Logistics - Durban Harbour, Africa's busiest container port
  • Manufacturing - Automotive, chemicals, textiles, and engineering
  • Sugar & Agriculture - Sugar cane, subtropical fruits, and timber
  • Tourism - Durban beaches, Drakensberg, Zulu cultural tourism
  • Mining - Coal, heavy minerals, and sand mining
  • Transport & Distribution - N3 corridor logistics hub

Popular Services: Import/export registration, CIDB for construction, COIDA for manufacturing, B-BBEE for tourism sector, company registration for logistics companies.

Business Opportunities in KwaZulu-Natal

KZN offers unique opportunities as South Africa's gateway to Africa:

  • Durban Port Opportunities - Import/export and freight forwarding
  • KZN Provincial Tenders - Government procurement
  • eThekwini Metro Tenders - Durban municipality projects
  • Dube TradePort & TradeZone - Special economic zone
  • Tourism Development - Coastal and Drakensberg regions
  • Manufacturing Sector - Automotive and chemicals

We help you: Register on CSD, prepare tender documents, obtain B-BBEE certificates, and comply with import/export and manufacturing regulations.
