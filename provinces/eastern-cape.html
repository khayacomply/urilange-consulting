Business Registration Services in Eastern Cape

We provide comprehensive business registration and compliance services across the Eastern Cape province. From Port Elizabeth to East London, Mthatha to Grahamstown, we're here to help your business succeed in this vibrant province.

Key Industries in Eastern Cape

  • Automotive Manufacturing - Major hub for VW, Ford, and suppliers
  • Port & Logistics - Port Elizabeth and East London harbors
  • Agriculture - Citrus, wool, mohair, and livestock
  • Tourism - Wild Coast, Addo Elephant Park
  • Renewable Energy - Wind farms along the coast
  • Construction - Infrastructure development

Popular Services: Company registration for automotive suppliers, COIDA for manufacturing, B-BBEE for tourism sector, CIDB for construction projects.

Business Opportunities in Eastern Cape

The Eastern Cape offers unique opportunities for entrepreneurs:

  • Coega IDZ - Industrial Development Zone opportunities
  • Automotive Supplier Park - Components manufacturing
  • Wild Coast Tourism - Growing eco-tourism sector
  • Eastern Cape Provincial Tenders - Government procurement
  • Nelson Mandela Bay Metro Tenders
  • SEDA & NYDA Support Programs

We help you: Register on CSD, prepare tender documents, obtain B-BBEE certificates, and comply with all requirements.
