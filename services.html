16 COMPREHENSIVE BUSINESS SERVICES

We offer end-to-end business registration and compliance solutions across all South African provinces

Company Registration & Compliance

CIPC Company Registration

Register your company with CIPC quickly and professionally. We handle Pty Ltd, NPC, and all company types.

Company Amendments

Amend company details with CIPC quickly and easily. Change directors, address, or company name.

Director Changes

Add, remove or update directors with CIPC compliance. Fast and efficient processing.

Annual Returns

File your annual returns on time and keep your company compliant. Avoid penalties and deregistration.

Tax & Regulatory Registration

Tax Clearance PIN

Apply for your Tax Clearance Pin for compliance and tenders. Essential for doing business with government.

SARS Income Tax Registration

Register with SARS for Income Tax as an individual or company. We handle all SARS requirements.

VAT Registration

Register for VAT and stay compliant with SARS regulations. Mandatory for businesses exceeding threshold.

UIF Registration

Register your business and employees with UIF. Compliance with unemployment insurance regulations.

COIDA Registration

Register with COIDA for workplace injury and disease cover. Protect your business and employees.

Share Certificates

Get professional share certificates for your company. Proper documentation for shareholders.

Business Growth & Opportunities

B-BBEE Affidavits & Certificates

Get your B-BBEE Affidavit or Certificate to unlock opportunities. Essential for government tenders.

Business Plans

Professional business plans for funding, investors and growth. Bank-ready documents.

CIDB Registration

Register with CIDB and get your grading for construction work. Mandatory for construction tenders.

CSD Registration

Register on the Central Supplier Database (CSD) for government tenders. Required for all state contracts.

Tender Documents

We prepare compliant tender documents that meet requirements. Increase your success rate.

SETA & QCTO Accreditation

We assist with SETA and QCTO accreditation to help you deliver training legally and successfully.
